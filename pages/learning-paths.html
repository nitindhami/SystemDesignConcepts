Learning Paths

Pick a guided route based on your target companies and current confidence level.

From LLD to HLD: Mindset Shift

  • LLD: class responsibilities, object lifecycle, interfaces, extension points.
  • HLD: services, data flow, reliability, scalability, operational risk.
  • Bridge: design APIs and data contracts that map cleanly from object models to distributed components.
Keep your path practical: learn, sketch, discuss tradeoffs, repeat.
